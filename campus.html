Campus & Facilities

A sprawling 15+ acre campus on NH-4 Tumkur Road designed to inspire learning, foster growth, and prepare you for the real world.

Our Campus

A Modern Campus Built for Excellence

Spread across 15+ acres along the prestigious NH-4 Tumkur Road in Nelamangala, the Vidhya Shekhar campus combines natural beauty with modern infrastructure. Lush greenery, well-maintained gardens, and open spaces provide the perfect backdrop for focused academic pursuit.

Every building on campus has been thoughtfully designed to support active learning. From simulation laboratories to smart classrooms, from a well-stocked library to our own teaching hospital, we have invested in facilities that match the best institutions in Karnataka.

Facilities

World-Class Infrastructure

Everything a student needs under one campus

Modern Campus

A beautifully planned 15+ acre campus located on NH-4 Tumkur Road. Surrounded by lush greenery and open landscapes, the environment fosters concentration and holistic development for every student.

Library & Digital Resources

An extensive collection of textbooks, reference materials, and peer-reviewed journals. Digital access to online databases, quiet study spaces, and dedicated reading rooms support self-paced learning.

Hostel Accommodation

Separate hostels for boys and girls with 2-sharing, 4-sharing, and 6-sharing options. Accommodation at ₹33,000/year and nutritious meals at ₹35,000/year. Round-the-clock security and warden supervision.

Advanced Laboratories

State-of-the-art simulation labs and practical laboratories specifically designed for nursing, allied health sciences, and paramedical programmes. Hands-on training with modern medical equipment and models.

Smart Classrooms

Air-conditioned classrooms equipped with digital projectors, modern furniture, and interactive smart boards. Designed to support engaging, technology-enhanced teaching and collaborative learning.

V.S Cafeteria

A spacious, hygienic cafeteria serving nutritious and affordable meals throughout the day. Diverse menu options cater to different dietary preferences, keeping students well-nourished and energised.

Northwest Hospital

Our own teaching hospital and clinical training partner right on campus. Students gain real patient exposure, attend clinical rotations, and develop bedside skills under expert supervision from day one.

BGS Hospital Partnership

In addition to Northwest Hospital, BGS Hospital serves as a clinical partner for advanced training opportunities. Students benefit from exposure to a wider range of specialisations and patient cases.

Transport Facility

Dedicated bus service connecting the campus to major parts of Bengaluru and surrounding areas. Conveniently located on NH-4 Tumkur Road, the campus is well-connected by public and private transport.

Sports & Recreation

Expansive sports grounds, indoor game facilities, and regular cultural events ensure students maintain a healthy balance between academics and recreation. Annual sports meets and inter-college competitions.

Why Our Campus

Designed for Holistic Development

At Vidhya Shekhar, we believe a great campus does more than house classrooms. It shapes experiences, builds character, and creates lifelong memories. Our campus has been designed with this philosophy at its core.

  • Own teaching hospital — clinical training from the very first year
  • Separate boys & girls hostels — safe, comfortable, well-supervised
  • Smart classrooms — projector-equipped, air-conditioned spaces
  • Simulation labs — practice on modern medical equipment
  • Green campus — 15+ acres of lush, well-maintained grounds
  • NH-4 connectivity — easy access from Bengaluru & Tumkur
