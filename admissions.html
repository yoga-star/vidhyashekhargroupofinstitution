Checklist

Documents Required

Please keep the following documents ready before visiting the campus or submitting your application online. Original documents are required for verification; photocopies will be retained.

  • Academic Mark Sheets — 10th (SSLC), 12th (PUC/+2), and any higher qualification
  • Photo ID Proof — Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, or Passport
  • Passport-size Photographs — 6 recent colour photographs (white background)
  • Transfer Certificate (TC) — from the last institution attended
  • Migration Certificate — if applicable (for inter-university transfers)
  • Caste / Category Certificate — if claiming reservation benefits
  • Income Certificate — if applying for fee concession or scholarship
