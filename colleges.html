Vidhya Shekhar College of Nursing

Nursing is the backbone of every healthcare system, and our College of Nursing has built a reputation for producing confident, clinically skilled professionals who are ready to serve from their first day on the ward. Affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences (RGUHS), the college offers a structured progression from diploma to postgraduate level.

What sets our nursing college apart is the seamless integration of classroom theory with bedside training at Northwest Hospital, our own multi-speciality teaching hospital on campus. Students don't just study patient care in textbooks — they practice it under expert supervision across real departments, from emergency triage to post-operative recovery.

College of Allied Health Sciences

Modern medicine depends on far more than doctors and nurses. Behind every accurate diagnosis, every successful surgery, and every clear scan is an allied health professional whose expertise made it possible. Our College of Allied Health Sciences, affiliated to RGUHS, trains the specialists who form this essential layer of healthcare delivery.

Each programme follows a 3-year academic curriculum plus a 1-year internship, giving students a full twelve months of rotational clinical exposure in hospital departments, diagnostic centres, and speciality labs. Graduates emerge not as beginners but as trained practitioners ready to join hospitals, diagnostic chains, and research facilities across India and abroad.

RGUHS Affiliated

College of Physiotherapy

Physiotherapy is one of the fastest-growing healthcare professions in India, driven by rising awareness of rehabilitation, sports medicine, and preventive care. Our College of Physiotherapy, affiliated to RGUHS, prepares graduates who combine deep anatomical knowledge with advanced manual and electrotherapeutic techniques.

The Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT) is a 4-year programme followed by a mandatory 6-month clinical internship. During the internship, students rotate through orthopaedic, neurological, cardio-pulmonary, and sports rehabilitation departments — gaining real clinical hours that make them employable the moment they graduate.

Our physiotherapy lab is equipped with ultrasound therapy units, TENS machines, traction apparatus, exercise therapy setups, and gait analysis tools. Students train on real patients under faculty supervision at Northwest Hospital, learning to design treatment plans, track patient progress, and work as part of a multi-disciplinary medical team.

Paramedical Board of Karnataka

College of Paramedical Science

Not every healthcare career requires a science stream in PUC. Our College of Paramedical Science opens the door for SSLC / 10th pass students who want to enter the medical field through diploma-level training — a path that leads to stable, in-demand jobs across hospitals, diagnostic labs, and dialysis centres.

Affiliated to the Paramedical Board of Karnataka, each diploma programme runs for 3 years plus a 6-month clinical internship. The curriculum balances foundational medical theory with intensive practical sessions, ensuring students can operate laboratory equipment, handle imaging machines, assist in surgical theatres, and manage dialysis procedures with confidence and precision.

This is one of the most accessible healthcare pathways available in Karnataka, and our graduates consistently secure placements in multi-speciality hospitals, standalone diagnostic centres, and government health facilities.

Legal Education

College of Law

The legal profession is built on precision, persuasion, and principle. Our College of Law offers a 3-year LLB programme designed for graduates who want to enter the legal field — whether their ambition is to argue cases in court, advise corporations, draft policy, or serve in the judiciary.

The curriculum covers every major area of Indian law: Constitutional Law, Criminal Law, Contract Law, Property Law, Corporate Law, Environmental Law, Intellectual Property, and more. But we go beyond textbook instruction. Students participate in moot court competitions, legal aid clinics, court visits, and internships with practising advocates and law firms — developing the advocacy and research skills that separate competent lawyers from exceptional ones.

For students drawn to public service, our faculty provides dedicated preparation pathways for judicial services examinations and civil service law optional. For those inclined toward the corporate world, the programme builds a strong foundation in commercial and regulatory law that opens doors to corporate counsel roles, compliance teams, and legal consultancies.
