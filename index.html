About Us

Empowering Students with Knowledge & Skills

Vidhya Shekhar Group of Institutions, located on NH-4, Tumkur Road in Nelamangala, Bengaluru, is a premier educational institution recognised by the Government of Karnataka. Our group comprises six distinguished colleges offering programmes in Nursing, Allied Health Sciences, Physiotherapy, Paramedical Sciences, Arts, Science & Management, and Law.

Affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences (RGUHS), Bangalore City University, and the Paramedical Board of Karnataka, we deliver quality education through experienced faculty, modern infrastructure, and hands-on clinical training at our own Northwest Hospital.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What courses are offered at Vidhya Shekhar?

B.Sc Nursing, M.Sc Nursing (5 specialisations), GNM, BPT, Allied Health Sciences (CCT, ATOT, MIT, MLT, BOT), Paramedical Diplomas (MLT, MIT, OT&AT, DT), BBA, BCA, MBA, MCA, and LLB.

Which universities are your programmes affiliated to?

Nursing & health sciences — RGUHS. Management — Bangalore City University & AICTE. Paramedical diplomas — Paramedical Board of Karnataka.

Is hostel accommodation available?

Yes. Separate hostels for boys & girls with 2/4/6 sharing. B.Sc Nursing & GNM fees include hostel. Others: ₹33,000/yr accommodation + ₹35,000/yr food.

What is the eligibility for paramedical diplomas?

Minimum 10th / SSLC pass. Duration: 3 years + 6 months internship.

Do you offer placement assistance?

Yes. Dedicated placement cell connecting students with leading hospitals, healthcare organisations, IT companies & corporates. Regular campus drives & skill workshops.

What makes VSET unique for BBA/BCA students?

Unique add-ons: Aviation, Cinematography, Film Acting, Script Writing, Film Editing (with Elements of Cinema Film Academy), AI, Cyber Security, Digital Marketing, Data Science & more.
