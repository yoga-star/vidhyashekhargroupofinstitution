About Vidhya Shekhar Group of Institutions

A decade of academic excellence shaping healthcare professionals, business leaders, and legal practitioners in Karnataka.

Our Story

Who We Are

Vidhya Shekhar Group of Institutions stands as one of the most respected educational groups in North Bengaluru. Established under the Vidhya Shekhar Education Trust, our campus on NH-4, Tumkur Road in Nelamangala provides students a learning environment that blends academic rigour with real-world readiness.

What began as a single nursing college has grown into a multi-disciplinary group encompassing six specialised colleges — each with its own identity, dedicated faculty, and modern infrastructure. From healthcare and allied sciences to business management and law, our programmes prepare graduates who are career-ready from day one.

Recognised by the Government of Karnataka and affiliated to leading universities including RGUHS, Bangalore City University, and the Paramedical Board of Karnataka, our institution meets the highest standards of educational quality and compliance.

Our Vision

To emerge as a nationally recognised centre of academic excellence that produces compassionate healthcare professionals, innovative business leaders, and principled legal practitioners who serve society with integrity.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and high-quality education through expert faculty, industry partnerships, clinical training, and modern infrastructure — ensuring every student is empowered to achieve their professional aspirations.

Our Values

Excellence in education, ethical conduct, compassionate care, continuous innovation, and inclusive growth. We believe every student deserves the opportunity to build a meaningful, fulfilling career.
