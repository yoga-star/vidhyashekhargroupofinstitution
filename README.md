# Vidhya Shekhar Group of Institutions

Official website for Vidhya Shekhar Group of Institutions, Nelamangala, Bengaluru.

## About

A premier educational institution recognised by the Government of Karnataka, offering 20+ programmes across 6 colleges:

- **Vidhya Shekhar College of Nursing** (B.Sc Nursing, GNM, M.Sc Nursing)
- **College of Allied Health Sciences** (CCT, ATOT, MIT, MLT, BOT)
- **College of Physiotherapy** (BPT)
- **College of Paramedical Science** (Diploma MLT, MIT, OT&AT, DT)
- **College of Arts, Science & Management** (BBA, BCA, MBA, MCA)
- **College of Law** (LLB)

Affiliated to RGUHS, Bangalore City University, Paramedical Board of Karnataka, and approved by AICTE.
